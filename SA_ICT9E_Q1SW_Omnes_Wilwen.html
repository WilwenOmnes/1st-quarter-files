What is Blox Fruits?

Blox Fruits
is an online fighting game with hundreds of different setups and builds for your liking.
The game requires a metric ton of grinding and thus people cheat often by getting bot
accounts. This game has something known as devil fruits and each fruit has different
passives and abilities to help you max out really quick. These passives are different for
each fruit and even the worst abilities can still be used due to its incredible passive.

My favorite thing to do in Blox fruits is pvp, and due to pvp, I need to learn blox fruits comboes

Blox Fruit Comboes are

  • hard
  • Fun(when landed)
  • VERY annoying to the opponent
  • Killers of time
  • Incredible HP drain
  • unique to its user

Each combo is different for each build. There is no "real" 
best combo in the game, though there are some comboes 
that perform WAY BETTER than others.

For example: (I will be using the actual terms that the community also uses).

  1. Kitsune C + X
  2. Godhuman X + Godhuman Z + Godhuman C
  3. CDK X + CDK Z(hold)
  4. Soul Guitar Z + Soul Guitar X
  5. (Aim at player) Kitsune Z + Kitsune F + Kitsune M1's.

You must also keep in mind to do ALL OF THESE STEPS IN
ORDER and in QUICK SUCCESSION to land the combo.
